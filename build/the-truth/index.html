1.1. The Start

1.1. The Start

This journey is an important one, the most important of my life as I do not know whether I will return or not. Someone like Yuri Alekseyevich Gagarin would have felt it as the first person going to space. It causes a twister in your mind and a tsunami in your gut. However, I must embark on this journey as there is no other choice. Since there is no other choice the only one must be taken. I calmed my mind that why do you worry about this life. There is no one to cry if you die. The world will go on without anyone every remembering you, after all they care about the results of your actions and not about you as a person or your opinion. You know what happened when you had money, and you lost it.

So now the fear of losing life is gone. Focus returned on task at hand. To fix the nonsense of this world I need a hero of highest quality. A champion of all times. Who can be such person? To figure that out I asked myself what is most important? The answer is survival. Survival is existence and existence is truth. Truth is the only thing which exists and eternal. So I need a champion of truth. Now it became easy that I need to go to Harishchandra. Only Harishchandra is fit to rule and no one else. He must help me.

Now that I have my first destination so I hopped on my time machine and in an instant I was with Harishchandra when he had just asked her wife for half of her Sari(a five yard single piece cloth Indian women wear), as funeral charge to burn their son. It was painful to watch. I am from an era where duties are ignored and corruption is rampant and here is this man asking her own wife for half of what she is wearing. Time stood still. I was filled with extreme sorrow for current state of earth and at the same time I was joyous that I chose the right champion. Never felt like this before. Well, Gods appeared and all that happened which you know. As soon as Gods allowed all beings on earth to heaven with body I approached Harishchandra. I introduced myself and said that your job is not done yet. I am from Kaliyuga and have come seeking his help to prevent humanity from disaster. I asked him that he must travel with me on this journey to future, so that mankind must be saved from disaster. He readily agreed without hesitation and said that I am very brave to have risked my life this way.
