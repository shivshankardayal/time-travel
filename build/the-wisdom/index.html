2.1. Seeking Wisdom

Now that I have a champion and a wise king I asked him that which other person should we take along. He answered he has lived only in Satya Yuga and all beings are their best. He has not seen other yugas and know other people from them. However, we need a person of immense knowledge. With this clue it was not hard to decided that we needed Ravana. I told him that we need Ravana. I had to tell him all the story of Ramayana. Since Gods were present I asked Lord Vishnu whether he will help us. Lord Vishnu refused. He said that you have free will and as Gods they have given intellect and wisdom to us. We should not expect Gods for any help. We have already been given what we deserve and will be given in future. He also said that Gods will not interfere in kali Yuga at all in human actions. That made sense. That also meant that Hanuman and Lord Rama are out of question. So we agreed that we need Ravana. Now the problem is how and when do we approach him. This is a grave question because he will not help easily. Harishchandra told me that since I have the knowledge of history I have to find the solution as he does not know future. He also said that it is my quest so I got to find a way.

We need a plan to approach him at right time so that Ravana helps us. I needed time to make a plan and when you have a time travel machine time is never a problem. So to solve this problem I had to ask myself was he born evil? No. He was born an innocent child. So why did he follow the evil path? He was so learned. The answer is not hard to find. The answer lies in the fact that he was discriminated and feared for his knowledge. Due to his knowledge and power he became arrogant and chose wrong path. Arroganc of power and money cannot be reasoned with. Arrogance of shallow knowledge can be eaily broken. However, he was learned and like a truly learned man he did not have pride about his knowledge. I knew because he helped Rama his arch enemy please Durga for winning the war against him. He very well knew that if does not choose evil path he will not die and remain immortal. So he chose to die at the hands of God. However, this was an opportunity to also fix the whole Sita kidnapping and save all who died. Also, we may need Bali and others so I decided to approach him when he completes his education and has not acquired any powers from Gods yet.

2.1.1. The Argument

And with a boom we arrived to Ravana as he had just completed his education and coming out of Gurukula. I am no coward but facing Ravana was an uphill task. He was shining in the prime of his youth. The life of gurukul had him appear like a living God. I was nervous but the presence of Harishchandra gave me the courage to face him. I also gave myself a bit of boost with the fact that even Ravana does not know what will happen in future but I do. So the plan is cooked and ready for execution.

Ravana saw us and he was surprised to see me with my funny modern dress of half pant and a T-shirt.
