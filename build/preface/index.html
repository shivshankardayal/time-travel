Preface

I am a very unhappy person and am tired of all the idiocy going on in this world. Since I cannot fix all this I am going to do this in my virtual world with my imaginary time machine. You are most welcome on this journey of time travel. Know this that no stone will be left unturned to make this jounrney pleasant, peaceful and enjoyable. After all I got to fix all the problems. Some of it will be fun and some of it will be pure nonsense. It is purely imaginary and none of it is true. So sit back, get your favorite cold drink and enjoy.
